Who we are

We are the Golden Valley HS School Alumni Association, a network of thoughts united by a shared history and a common purpose.

GOLDEN VALLEY HS SCHOOL

Golden Valley HS School (GVHS) is an outstanding educational institution located in Dharmanagar, Tripura. Founded on June 30, 1989, it became Govt. Aided in 2002 and CBSE affiliated in 2020. Today it serves over 1500 students.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

to foster lifelong relationships between the institution and its graduates, creating a vibrant community that benefits both current students and the school itself.

Our Vision

becoming a global, interconnected community that fosters lifelong connections between the institution and its graduates, creating a network of loyalty, mentorship, and mutual support to enhance academic excellence, promote professional growth, and give back to current students, the school, and society at large.

Our Values

Community and Belonging

Alumni groups create a sense of shared experience and instant connection, offering a strong support system and a place to maintain friendships.

Mentorship and Guidance

Experienced alumni can provide valuable insights, advice, and guidance for navigating career challenges and achieving personal growth.

Professional Networking

Groups organize events and platforms that connect graduates with experienced professionals, industry leaders, and other alumni for career growth and collaboration.

Lifelong Learning

Alumni can access tailored learning programs, academic networks, and the prestige of their alma mater's reputation, which enhances career success.

Community & Social Responsibility

By mentoring younger people, alumni demonstrate a positive influence on their communities and contribute to the enhancement of their alma mater's social unity

Integrity

Alumni groups act as a bridge, connecting the past, present, and future, ensuring a lasting relationship between graduates and their educational institution.
